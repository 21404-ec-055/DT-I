Bungalow Subtypes

California Bungalow
A stylish, low-profile bungalow that blends modern living with vintage charm. Features include a private garden, a cozy porch, premium interiors, and a backyard pool, making it perfect for relaxed and luxurious suburban life.
American Craftmen
Known for its handcrafted woodwork and classic American design, this single-story bungalow offers wide front porches, exposed beams, spacious interiors, and a warm, welcoming feel—great for family-oriented lifestyles.
Chicago Bungalow
Inspired by early 20th-century designs, the Chicago Bungalow features a brick exterior, sun-filled living rooms, elegant archways, and compact yet comfortable layout—perfect for city dwellers wanting both charm and function.
