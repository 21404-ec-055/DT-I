Individual House Subtypes

Single Family
A cozy and private standalone house perfect for individuals or couples. It typically includes a single bedroom, hall, and kitchen, ideal for minimal maintenance and peaceful living.
Town House
A modern and compact two-bedroom home, often located in urban or semi-urban settings. Offers a good balance of space, privacy, and affordability for small families or working professionals.
Multi Family
A spacious residence designed for multiple families or a large extended family. Comes with three or more bedrooms, ample living space, multiple bathrooms, and often includes amenities like a backyard or garage.
