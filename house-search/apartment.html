Apartment Subtypes

High Rise Apartments
Perfect for singles or couples, these apartments are located in towering buildings with breathtaking views, 24/7 security, elevators, and easy access to urban amenities. Great choice for city dwellers looking for convenience and affordability.
Garden Apartments
These apartments offer direct access to a garden or green space, ideal for small families or nature lovers. They feature two well-ventilated bedrooms, a living area, and a cozy layout perfect for peaceful residential living.
RailRoad Apartments
Known for their linear layout, these spacious apartments are great for medium to large families. Each room connects directly to the next, ensuring efficient space usage while maintaining privacy and comfort.
