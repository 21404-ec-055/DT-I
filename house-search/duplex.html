Duplex Subtypes

Low Rise Duplex
A two-floor residential unit perfect for growing families, offering privacy and comfort. Includes spacious bedrooms, a cozy living room, modular kitchen, and a small backyard, ideal for peaceful suburban living with easy access.
Standard Duplex
A modern duplex designed with sleek interiors, premium fittings, and an open-concept layout. It features multiple bedrooms, attached bathrooms, private terrace, and an elegant staircase connecting both levels—perfect for stylish living.
Ground Duplex
A duplex built starting from the ground level with direct access to a garden or patio. Offers spacious ground-floor common areas and private upper-level bedrooms—blending comfort, functionality, and natural surroundings beautifully.
